Method

Founders

The people shaping Red Seal English.

Founder

Alan Moura

Alan Moura is the founder of Red Seal English, where he helps international professionals speak English with clarity and confidence in real-world situations. With over 14 years of experience working directly with students, he saw firsthand that most people weren’t struggling with grammar — they were struggling to communicate in meaningful contexts.

That insight led him to build Red Seal English around practical, results-driven training that focuses on how English is actually used in professional and everyday life in English-speaking countries like Canada. His work is focused on helping ambitious individuals move past hesitation and express themselves clearly when it matters most.

Co-founder

Giovanna de Queiróz

Giovanna de Queiróz is responsible for the administrative side of Red Seal English, a school she co-founded alongside Alan. While the academic vision is led by her partner, Giovanna focuses on building and managing the structure that supports each student’s journey.

With over 10 years of experience in educational administration, she has led departments such as admissions, finance, and HR. She brings this experience to Red Seal with a clear goal: to support every student with care, attention, and efficiency.

Giovanna is also actively involved in developing and testing new projects and ideas, always seeking ways to improve and evolve the student experience. For her, great education goes beyond the classroom: it’s about creating an environment where every detail matters and every student feels valued.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question?

What class times are available?

We offer a variety of schedule options, so you can pick times that fit your routine.

Do students receive a certificate?

Yes. A certificate is provided once you complete the course or levels.

Are the lessons live or recorded?

All lessons are fully live, allowing real-time interaction with teachers and classmates.

Is this program suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. The course is designed for all levels, including complete beginners, and you’ll be placed accordingly.

Will I be placed with students at the same level?

Yes. Groups are organized by proficiency level to ensure a better learning experience.

Is the course focused on speaking skills?

Yes. The method emphasizes practical communication and speaking confidence.

How much does the course cost?

Pricing may vary depending on the plan or current offers. For the most accurate details, you’ll need to check the enrollment page or contact the team.
